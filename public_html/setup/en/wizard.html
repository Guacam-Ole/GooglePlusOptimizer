wham.png
Filter
Wham - Filter
At christmas the constant Wham-posts can be a bit annoying. At least at google+ you can remove these posts.
Decide what you want to filter from Wham. (Just don't activate any of this if you still want your daily "Last Christmas" dose.

  Filter "WHAM" in post
If anyone writes down the word "WHAM" this post will disappear in your stream.

  Filter "WHAM" in url
If the word "WHAM" appears in a link to another page this post will disappear in your stream.

  Filter "Last Christmas" in post
If anyone writes down the words "Last Christmas" this post will disappear in your stream.

  Filter "Last Christmas" in url
If the words "Last Christmas" appears in a link to another page this post will disappear in your stream.

Bookmarks
Bookmarks allow you to save a local copy of posts you like most. Even if these posts disappear from Google+ (maybe because they are deleted) you still have your local copy. To use the interactive features (like commenting oder sharing) click onto the date of that entry. Saved Bookmarks can be accessed via the "star"-symbol next to your notification bell.
