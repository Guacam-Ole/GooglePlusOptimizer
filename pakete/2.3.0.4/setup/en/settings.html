Google+ - filter settings

On the left side you can see which filters are enabled. If the symbol is black-white, the filter is disabled. Just click onto the picture to enable it. The picture changes to the coloured version.

Just move over the picture with the mouse to get more information on each filter. The filters "hashtag" and "custom" have also be to customized on the advanced settings.

All filters will be saved immediatly.
